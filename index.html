Staffing Transparency Suite

Choose the right staffing tool

The suite uses CMS Payroll-Based Journal staffing data, CMS Provider Information, CMS Quality Measures Claims, and CMS SNF Enrollment affiliation context to help users review Connecticut nursing home staffing patterns with care.

Read before using: PBJ staffing is quarterly screening data. CT comparison points are PBJ-derived screening estimates, not formal DPH compliance findings. Case-mix comparison points and CMS quality measures are contextual CMS values. Ownership and affiliation context is informational and does not prove common day-to-day control. Use drill-down tools before drawing conclusions.
