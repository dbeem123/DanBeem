Connecticut Nursing Home Ownership & Staffing Explorer

This tool groups Connecticut nursing homes by CMS SNF Enrollment affiliation entity where available, then summarizes PBJ staffing patterns across facilities connected to the same affiliation name.

Staffing, Provider Information, and SNF Enrollment affiliation fields are screening and context data. A shared affiliation entity does not by itself prove common day-to-day operations, poor care, harm, neglect, or regulatory violations.

Interpretation

How to read the group view

What this group-level view shows

It shows staffing patterns for Connecticut facilities linked to the same CMS SNF Enrollment affiliation entity, including quarterly group averages and facility-by-facility variation.

What it may suggest

It may identify patterns worth closer review, such as consistently lower or higher staffing, reliance on contract staff, or differences among facilities connected to the same affiliation name.

What it cannot prove

It cannot prove common control, uniform operations, poor care, neglect, harm, or violations. These data points are best used to identify questions, then paired with resident experience, survey findings, complaints, ownership records, and other evidence.

Verification Note

Sources and limits

This static view derives affiliation groups from the generated Connecticut staffing export. It uses CMS Payroll-Based Journal staffing, CMS Nursing Home Provider Information, and CMS Skilled Nursing Facility Enrollments where available.

Case-mix benchmarks and Connecticut direct-care comparison fields are contextual screening points. They do not replace the actual PBJ staffing values and do not determine legal compliance.
