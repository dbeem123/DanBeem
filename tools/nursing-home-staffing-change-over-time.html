Interpretation

How to read this change view

Changes are not causes

A decline or improvement is a change in PBJ-reported staffing metrics. This tool does not explain why staffing changed.

Crossings are screening indicators

Threshold crossings use the PBJ-derived Connecticut direct-care comparison estimate. They are not formal DPH compliance findings.

Review full facility context

Use "View facility trend" to inspect all five quarters, ownership/affiliation context, case-mix comparison, and caveats before drawing conclusions.
