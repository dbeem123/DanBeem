## 10. Short Notes On How These Fit Together

- `ACL Tables 1-3` and `NORS Parts 1-4` are the top NORS reporting/coding layer.
- `FAQs`, `answer sheets`, and `user-friendly complaint-code materials` are the ambiguity/QA layer.
- `Initial certification curriculum modules` are the workflow/documentation layer.
- `42 CFR Part 483` and `Appendix PP` are the regulatory context layer.
- `Connecticut statutes/regulations/resources` are the state overlay layer.
- `45 CFR Part 1324` is the ombudsman program authority layer.
- `CT AI governance pages` are the responsible-use/governance layer for any state deployment conversation.
