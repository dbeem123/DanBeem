Interpretation

How to read this table

What lower estimates may suggest

Lower staffing estimates may warrant closer review, especially when they repeat across quarters or appear alongside resident concerns, complaint patterns, survey findings, or high-acuity needs.

What below-comparison flags mean

Below-comparison flags are PBJ-derived screening indicators against the Connecticut direct-care comparison points. They are not formal DPH compliance findings.

What to do next

Use "View facility details" to inspect the facility's trend, ownership/affiliation context, case-mix comparison, and reporting caveats before drawing conclusions.
